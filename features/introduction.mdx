## What is Nobly Insight?

Nobly Insight is a modern, web-based content services and case management platform. It brings together document management, business process automation, smart search, and AI-assisted productivity in a single, secure, multi-tenant solution that runs in the cloud or in your own private environment.

This section gives you a high-level view of what the platform can do and what your teams get out of the box. Each feature area links to its own page for more detail.

## At a glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **What it is** | A web platform for managing documents, cases, and business processes — with built-in AI |
| **Who it's for** | Banks, insurance companies, pension funds, public-sector organisations, and any business that handles large volumes of documents and structured cases |
| **How users access it** | Modern web browser, Microsoft Office add-ins, and Office Online editing |
| **Languages** | English, Danish, and Norwegian (Bokmål) — additional languages on request |
| **Hosting** | Cloud-hosted, private cloud, or hybrid |
| **Authentication** | Single sign-on via your existing identity provider (Microsoft Entra ID, Azure AD, or other OpenID Connect / OAuth 2.0 providers) |
| **AI processing** | Open-weight models on Nobly-owned servers in EU co-location facilities — see [How we use AI](/ai/introduction) |

## Why customers choose Nobly Insight

- **One platform** instead of separate tools for documents, cases, search, and AI.
- **AI that respects security** — every AI feature honours each user's access rights, runs on Nobly-owned infrastructure in the EU, and uses open-weight models. No customer data leaves Nobly to be processed by a third-party AI provider, and no customer data is used to train models.
- **Configuration over code** — administrators can model new document types, business objects, forms, and lookups without developer effort.
- **Safe extensibility** — when code is needed, the script engine offers a sandboxed, versioned, audited way to add it.
- **Built for regulated industries** — granular security, full audit trails, and multi-tenant isolation.
- **Modern user experience** — fast, responsive web UI with full keyboard support, dark mode, internationalisation, and accessibility in mind.
- **Rich integrations** — Outlook and Office add-ins, Office Online co-editing, and a complete REST API for everything else.
